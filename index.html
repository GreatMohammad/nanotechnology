Why Low-Cost Nanotechnology?

The semiconductor industry accounted for over half a trillion USD in value in 2024, creating tremendous job opportunities worldwide. However, the high barrier to entry for nanotechnology education has prevented many institutions and students from accessing this field. This manual changes that by providing complete laboratory experiences for under $10,000 total.

All experiments in this manual are designed to be as representative as possible of real industry processes while remaining budget-friendly and accessible.
